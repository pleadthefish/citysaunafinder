Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

Privacy First: CitySaunaFinder is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information and process most data locally in your browser.

Information We Collect

CitySaunaFinder collects very limited information to provide our service:

Location Data: When you use our location-based search, we may access your approximate location through your browser's geolocation API. This information is used solely to show nearby saunas and is not stored on our servers or transmitted to third parties.

Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, such as popular search terms and general geographic regions of interest.

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used exclusively to:

• Display relevant sauna locations near your current location
• Improve our search algorithms and user experience
• Provide customer support when requested

Important: Location processing happens primarily in your browser. We do not store your precise location or create user profiles.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for analytical purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with certain third-party services to provide functionality:

Google Maps API: We use Google Maps to display sauna locations and provide directions. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

Cloudflare: Our website is hosted through Cloudflare, which may collect standard web server logs. Please see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information we process. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Since we collect minimal personal information, most data processing happens locally on your device. You can:

• Disable location services in your browser to prevent location access
• Clear your browser cache to remove any locally stored data
• Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: privacy@citysaunafinder.com
