The Experience

Olympus Spa brings the authentic Korean jjimjilbang tradition to the Pacific Northwest. Since 2015, this women-only facility has offered a comprehensive thermal wellness experience rooted in centuries-old Korean healing practices.

The spa's centerpiece is its collection of six distinct thermal rooms, each maintained at specific temperatures and featuring different natural materials‚Äîjade, clay, Himalayan salt, and more. Together with hydrotherapy pools and a eucalyptus-infused steam room, these spaces create a complete journey through heat, cold, and rest.

What distinguishes Olympus Spa is its attention to the traditional Korean approach: cycling through extreme temperatures, taking time to rest, and allowing the body to heal through thermal contrast. This isn't rushed wellness‚Äîit's meant to be savored over hours.
